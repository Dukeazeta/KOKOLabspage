New App Release

⏱️ Introducing TimerKoko: Your New Productivity Companion

We're thrilled to announce the release of TimerKoko, our latest minimalist productivity app! Designed with simplicity and efficiency in mind, TimerKoko helps you manage your time effectively with its elegant interface and powerful features.

Key Features

  • Simple Timer Interface: A clean, distraction-free design that lets you focus on what matters - your time.
  • Customizable Presets: Create and save your favorite timer durations for quick access.
  • Background Operation: Timer continues running even when the app is in the background.
  • Notification Alerts: Get notified when your timer completes, even if the app isn't active.
  • Dark Mode Support: Easy on the eyes with automatic theme switching based on system settings.

Why TimerKoko?

In today's fast-paced world, managing time effectively is crucial. Whether you're following the Pomodoro Technique, timing your workouts, or managing cooking times, TimerKoko is designed to be your reliable companion. Its minimalist approach eliminates distractions while providing all the essential features you need in a timer app.
